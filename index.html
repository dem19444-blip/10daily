    App Instructions

    Tailor Monday

    Welcome to 10Daily

    The Philosophy

    This app is designed for runners to build strength and resilience in just 10 minutes Daily. Each session consists of 5 exercises repeated through 3 sets (30s work / 10s rest).

    How to Use

    Start: Hit 'Start' to begin the 10-second 'Get Ready' countdown. There will be 3 second Beep countdowns for each change between 'work' and 'rest'. The highlighted exercise is the current or upcoming exercise.

    Tailor: Want to change an exercise? Tap 'Tailor' to swap any movement from our runner-specific exercise pool or add your own 'Custom' exercise. You can tailor the length of work and rest also, although bear in mind that 30s work / 10s rest is perfect for keeping your workout to 10 minutes exactly. You can also change the 'focus' of each day, however, the app is pre-populated with the perfect balance for building runner strength and resilience.

    Progression: Your progress is tracked on the Weekly Progress page, click below the day of the week to access this. Aim to get 6 ticks by Sunday! Use the 'Reset Week' button on Sunday to start fresh, or use Sunday as a catch-up day to complete your week.

    iPhone & Audio Tips

    Music: You can run Spotify or Apple Music in the background. 10Daily beeps will play over your music without pausing it.

    Installation: For the best experience, tap the 'Share' icon in Safari and select 'Add to Home Screen' to use 10Daily as a full-screen app.

    The Exercises

    Our routines focus on Single-leg Strength (running power), Core Stability (form maintenance), and Hip/Glute activation (injury prevention).
